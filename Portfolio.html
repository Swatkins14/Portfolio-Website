About Me

I graduated from Leeds Arts University in 2021 after completing a BA (Hons) Filmmaking degree. In my time at university I worked on a number of projects in various roles. I specialised in producing as this was where I found my talents lied, below you will find some of the projects I undertook on my course.


Film Projects

Parcels (2018)

Writer/Director

In 2018, I wrote and directed the film 'Parcels,' undertaking a range of responsibilities. These encompassed casting, crew coordination, on-set direction, and post-production organization.

Villmore (2020)

Producer/Director

In 2020, I worked in collaboration with a team to create a documentary focused around a dancer called Villmore, this was a piece about how his age didn't stop him from pushing himself and continuing his dance journey.

Why the Arts? (2021)

Producer/Director

In 2021, I produced and directed a documentary series involving two different artists and their reasons for choosing the arts, why they enjoy it and their opinions on the benefits of art in society.
